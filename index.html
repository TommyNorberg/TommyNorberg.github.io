    • Level Up - Scott Rogers
    • Pattern Language for Game Design - Chris Barney
    • Patterns in Game Design - Staffan Bjork
    • Proffessional Techniques for Cideo Game Writing - Wendy Despain
    • Reality is Broken - Jane McGonigal

    • Slay the Dragon: Writing Great Video Games - Denton Bryant & Keith Giglio
